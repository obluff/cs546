South Shore Charter Public School

Ahh charter schools, the injection of the market into our public school system. School choice, an embodiment of the neoliberal ethos, garners a lot of admittedly fair criticism. However, in my case it provided a chill, diverse, progressive alternative to to the low performing schools in my district. It was odd at some times but my time there was reasonable.
